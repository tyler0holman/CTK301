Introduction

Tyler Holman (also known by his artist name and online handle "bingo__") is a music producer based out of Illinois. Influenced by an eclectic musical taste, as well as a passion for video games and their music, their works have a bold and unique flavor all their own. Tyler is currently studying at Illinois State University in the Creative Technologies program.
